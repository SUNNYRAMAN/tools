Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about signing PDFs

Is it legal to sign PDFs with this tool?

Yes, digital signatures created with our tool are legally binding for most documents. However, for highly sensitive legal documents, you may need specialized digital certificates. Always check the specific requirements for your document type.

What file formats are supported for signature upload?

We support JPG, PNG, and GIF formats for signature images. For PDF files, we accept standard PDF format. The maximum file size for PDFs is 50MB.

Can I sign multiple pages in a PDF document?

Yes, you can add signatures to multiple pages. After applying the first signature, you'll have the option to add more signatures to other pages in your document.

Is my signature secure when using this tool?

Absolutely. We prioritize your privacy and security. All file processing happens securely, and we automatically delete your files from our servers after processing is complete. Your signature data is never stored.

Can I customize the appearance of my signature?

Yes, you can customize the size, position, and style of your signature. For typed signatures, you can choose from several handwriting-style fonts to make it look more natural.

Do I need to create an account to use this tool?

No account is required! You can sign PDFs immediately without any registration. We believe in providing instant access to our services.
