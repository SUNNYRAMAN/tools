Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about merging PDF files

Is it safe to merge PDF files with your tool?

Yes, absolutely. Our PDF merger works entirely in your browser, which means your files never leave your computer. We don't store or have access to your documents at any point.

How many PDF files can I merge at once?

You can merge as many PDF files as you want in one go. There are no limits on the number of files or total file size. However, very large files may take longer to process depending on your device's capabilities.

Can I rearrange the order of pages before merging?

Yes, you can customize the page order before merging. Our tool allows you to drag and drop files to rearrange their order, and you can also choose between sequential or custom ordering options.

What happens to bookmarks and annotations in the merged PDF?

By default, we preserve bookmarks and annotations. However, you can choose to remove annotations in the options panel if needed. We also offer an option to add new bookmarks for each merged file.

Do you add watermarks to the merged PDF?

No, we never add watermarks to your documents. The merged PDF will be completely clean and ready to use without any branding or limitations.
