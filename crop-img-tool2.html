Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats does the Crop Image Tool support?

Our Crop Image Tool supports JPG, JPEG, PNG, GIF, and WebP formats. These are the most common image formats used on the web and in digital photography.

Is there a file size limit for images?

We recommend keeping images under 10MB for optimal performance. Larger files may take longer to process depending on your device's capabilities.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, all image processing happens directly in your browser. Your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Can I crop images to specific dimensions?

Yes, you can either use the interactive crop area or input exact width and height values in pixels. You can also lock the aspect ratio for proportional cropping.

Does the tool work on mobile devices?

Yes, our Crop Image Tool is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones and tablets. The interface adapts to different screen sizes.

Is there any cost to use this tool?

No, our Crop Image Tool is completely free to use with no hidden costs, watermarks, or usage limits. We believe in providing accessible tools for everyone.
