Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about JPG to PDF conversion

How many JPG images can I convert at once?

You can convert up to 20 JPG images at once. Each image will be converted to a separate page in the PDF document, unless you choose the "Single Page" option.

What is the maximum file size for conversion?

The maximum file size for each JPG image is 10MB. If you need to convert larger files, consider compressing them first using our Image Compressor tool.

Will the quality of my images be preserved?

Yes, by default we preserve the original quality of your images. If you enable the compression option, we'll optimize the file size while maintaining good visual quality.

Can I rearrange the order of images in the PDF?

Yes, you can rearrange the order of images before conversion. Simply drag and drop the images in the file list to change their order in the final PDF.

Is there a limit to how many times I can use this tool?

No, there are no usage limits. You can convert as many JPG images to PDF as you need, completely free of charge.

What happens to my files after conversion?

Your privacy is important to us. All uploaded files are automatically deleted from our servers after 1 hour. We do not store or access your files for any other purpose.
