Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about signing PDFs

Is it legal to sign PDFs electronically?

Yes, in most countries, electronic signatures are legally binding for many types of documents. However, some documents like wills, real estate transfers, and certain court documents may require traditional handwritten signatures. Always check local regulations for specific requirements.

Are my documents secure when using this tool?

Absolutely. All processing happens directly in your browser - your PDFs are never uploaded to our servers. This ensures complete privacy and security for your documents.

What file formats are supported for signatures?

You can create signatures in three ways: draw directly on the canvas, type your name and choose from various cursive fonts, or upload an image of your signature (JPG, PNG, or GIF format).

Can I position the signature anywhere on the document?

Yes, you can drag and drop the signature to any position on the PDF page. You can also resize it to fit your needs.

Is there a file size limit for PDFs?

We recommend PDFs under 50MB for optimal performance. Larger files may take longer to process depending on your device's capabilities.

Do I need to create an account to use this tool?

No account is required! You can sign PDFs immediately without any registration. We believe in providing instant access to our tools.
