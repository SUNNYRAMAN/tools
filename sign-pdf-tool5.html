Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers about our PDF editing and signature tool

Is it really free to sign PDFs with your tool?

Yes, our PDF signature tool is completely free to use. You can draw, type, or upload signatures without any cost or watermarks.

Are the signatures legally binding?

In many jurisdictions, digital signatures created with our tool are legally binding for most documents. However, for highly sensitive documents like real estate transactions or wills, you may need a certified digital signature service.

Can I use this tool on mobile devices?

Yes, our tool is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones and tablets. The draw signature feature works particularly well with touchscreens.

Are my files secure when using your signature tool?

Absolutely. We prioritize your privacy and security. All file processing happens securely in your browser, and we automatically delete your files after processing. We never store your documents on our servers.

What is the maximum file size I can sign?

We support PDF files up to 100MB. For optimal performance, we recommend files under 50MB. If you have a larger file, consider compressing it first using our PDF compressor tool.

Can I add multiple signatures to one document?

Yes, you can add as many signatures as needed to your PDF document. Each signature can be positioned independently and saved for future use.
