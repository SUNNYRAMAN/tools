Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to sign PDFs online?

Yes, our PDF signing tool is completely safe. All processing happens directly in your browser, and your files are never uploaded to our servers. We don't store any of your documents or personal information.

What types of signatures can I add?

You can add three types of signatures: Draw your signature with your mouse or touchscreen, type your name and choose from different fonts, or upload an image of your signature.

Can I position the signature anywhere on the PDF?

Yes, after adding your signature, you can drag and drop it to any position on the PDF page. You can also resize it to fit your needs.

Is there a file size limit?

Yes, the maximum file size for PDFs is 25MB. If your file is larger, we recommend using our PDF Compressor tool first.

Do I need to create an account?

No, you don't need to create an account or register to use our PDF signing tool. It's completely free and works without any sign-up requirements.
