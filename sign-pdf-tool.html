Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about signing PDFs

Is it legal to sign PDFs with this tool?

Yes, digital signatures created with our tool are legally binding for most documents. However, for highly sensitive legal documents, we recommend using specialized e-signature services that provide additional authentication and verification.

Are my files secure when using this tool?

Absolutely. All file processing happens directly in your browser. Your PDF files and signatures never leave your device or get uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What file formats are supported for signatures?

You can draw your signature directly on the canvas, type it using various fonts, or upload an image of your signature in PNG, JPG, JPEG, or GIF format.

Can I position my signature anywhere on the PDF?

Yes, after adding your signature to the PDF, you can drag and drop it to any position on the page. You can also resize it to fit your needs.

Is there a limit to how many signatures I can add?

No, you can add as many signatures as you need to your PDF document. Each signature can be positioned independently on any page.

What is the maximum file size for PDFs?

We recommend PDF files up to 25MB for optimal performance. Larger files may take longer to process depending on your device's capabilities.
