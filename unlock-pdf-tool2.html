How to Unlock a PDF File

Follow these simple steps to remove password protection from your PDF documents:

  1. Upload your password-protected PDF file using the upload area above.
  2. Enter the password for the PDF file.
  3. Select your preferred permission settings for the unlocked PDF.
  4. Click the "Unlock PDF" button to process your file.
  5. Download your unlocked PDF file once processing is complete.
